Cardboard


Cardboard is a generic term for heavy-duty paper-based products having greater thickness
Features : Absorption power, Insulation qualities (heat, sound, vibration), Air permeability and Shape stability

Water-proof: No

Durability: Strong & Resilient

Availability: Commonly Available

Cost: Affordable & Cheap(80-150rs per sqft)

Eco-Friendly: It is 100% biodegradable, minimizes waste generation,recyclable

Glass


Glass is an inorganic solid material,usually transparent, hard, brittle,& impervious to natural elements
Features : Transparency, Heat resistance, Pressure,Breakage resistance and Chemical resistance

Water-proof: Yes

Durability: brittle, can fracture

Availability: Commonly Available

Cost: Affordable,based on quality prices will increase

Eco-Friendly: Requires less energy, Reusable, Causes CO2 emmissions
